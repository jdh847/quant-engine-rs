# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project is committed to a respectful, constructive, and inclusive community.
By participating, you agree to:

- Discuss ideas with technical rigor and respect.
- Assume good intent and avoid personal attacks.
- Keep feedback actionable and specific.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Threats, intimidation, or doxxing.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block contributors for severe or repeated violations.

## Reporting

Open a private security-style report in `SECURITY.md` contact channel for serious conduct incidents.
